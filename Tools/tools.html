My Crafting Tools

Dremel Multi-Tool & Attachments

My Dremel multi-tool is indispensable for intricate work on metal and wood. With various carving bits, sanding bands, and polishing attachments, it allows me to achieve fine details, smooth edges, and a brilliant shine on all my pieces. It's truly the workhorse of my workshop.

Morakniv Carving Knives

For precision carving and shaping, I rely on the exceptional quality of Morakniv knives. Their sharp, durable blades and comfortable handles are perfect for detail work in wood and other materials, allowing me to bring unique textures and forms to life.

Precision sharpening stones

Before polishing, fine hand files are crucial for shaping and removing imperfections from my metalwork. I use a variety of shapes and grits to refine each curve and angle, ensuring a flawless foundation for the final finish.

Many grades of sandpaper

Achieving the signature luster of FJORDCRAFTS pieces requires alott of sanding and oils to get the lasting shine.
